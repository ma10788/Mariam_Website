All About Deforestation

Why is Deforestation a Problem?

Deforestation is a serious issue affecting our planet. It leads to loss of biodiversity, disrupts ecosystems, and contributes to climate change.

Key Facts About Deforestation

  • 18 million acres of forests are lost each year.
  • Contributes to 15% of global greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Rainforests house over 50% of Earth's species.

What Can You Do?

Small actions can make a big difference. Reduce paper waste, support sustainable products, and donate to reforestation projects.
