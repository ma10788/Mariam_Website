FAQ Section

What is deforestation?

Deforestation is the clearing or thinning of forests by humans to make way for agriculture, urban use, or other purposes.

Why is deforestation harmful?

Deforestation disrupts ecosystems, contributes to climate change, and leads to the loss of biodiversity.

How can I help?

You can help by planting trees, reducing paper use, and supporting organizations that fight deforestation.
