All About Deforestation

Why is Deforestation a Problem?

Deforestation impacts biodiversity, ecosystems, and climate change. Forest loss disrupts habitats, increases carbon emissions, and destabilizes the environment.

Key Facts About Deforestation

  • 18 million acres of forests are lost annually—27 soccer fields per minute.
  • Deforestation contributes to 15% of global greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Rainforests host over 50% of Earth’s species.

What Can You Do?

Reduce paper waste, support sustainable products, and donate to reforestation efforts.

FAQ

What is the biggest cause of deforestation?

Agricultural expansion is the primary cause, including palm oil, soy, and cattle farming.
