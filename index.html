All About Deforestation

Why is Deforestation a Problem?

Deforestation leads to loss of habitat for millions of species, contributes to climate change, and disrupts the water cycle.

Causes of Deforestation

Some major causes include agriculture, logging, and urbanization.

Effects of Deforestation

Deforestation can lead to soil erosion, loss of biodiversity, and increased greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.
